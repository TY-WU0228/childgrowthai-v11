Privacy / Beta Note

ChildGrowth AI handles child-related information such as:
- Name / year level / school
- Homework photos
- Teacher comments
- Emotion records
- Weekly routines

For beta testing:
- Do not upload highly sensitive documents unless you are comfortable testing.
- AI observations are not medical, psychological, or educational diagnoses.
- For public launch, add login, per-family permissions, delete data, and a privacy policy.
